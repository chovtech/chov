## PHASE 4 — Launch Pages & JVZoo Setup

1. **Main sales page** (`usepagepersona.com`)
   The marketing page people land on before buying. Shows what PagePersona does, who it's for, what they get, and the price.

2. **FastPass page**
   Shown to buyers who skip the Bundle offer at checkout. A shorter offer at a lower price — last chance to get everything bundled before going through the full step-up pages.

3. **OTO pages (5 pages)**
   One page per upgrade offer shown after the main purchase:
   - OTO 1: Unlimited — remove all limits
   - OTO 2: Professional — remove PagePersona branding
   - OTO 3: Agency — manage clients under your own brand
   - OTO 4: DFY — Chike sets everything up for them (20 slots/month)
   - OTO 5: White Label Self-Hosted — full deployment on their own server

4. **Thank-you pages**
   Shown after each purchase. Tells the buyer what they just got and how to log in.

5. **Set everything up on JVZoo**
   Create the product listings, connect the payment links, set up the affiliate programme, configure the Bundle coupon code for the webinar, and schedule the webinar during the 5-day launch window.
   - Replace placeholder product IDs in `apps/pagepersona/backend/app/routers/webhooks.py` with real JVZoo product IDs after setup.
