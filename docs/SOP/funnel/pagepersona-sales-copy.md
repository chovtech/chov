## SECTION 11 — FEATURES DEEP DIVE

**Headline:**
> Here's Everything Inside PagePersona

---

**15 Visitor Signals — Know Exactly Who's On Your Page**

> PagePersona detects 15 different visitor signals in real time — and you can combine any of them with AND/OR logic to build rules as simple or as precise as you need.

- **Traffic source (UTM)** — fire rules based on utm_source, utm_medium, or utm_campaign. Show Facebook visitors a different headline to Google visitors. Show email subscribers something your cold traffic never sees.
- **Country** — serve location-specific offers, local social proof, or geo-targeted urgency. Resolved server-side — visitors can't spoof it.
- **Device type** — desktop, mobile, or tablet. Optimise your offer for how they're actually browsing.
- **Visit count** — first visit, second visit, fifth visit. Show new visitors proof. Show returning visitors a reason to stop hesitating.
- **Time on page** — fire a popup after 30 seconds. Insert a countdown after someone's been reading for a minute. Reward engaged visitors with an exclusive offer.
- **Scroll depth** — trigger actions when someone hits 50%, 75%, or 100% of your page. The people who scroll to the bottom are the most interested — treat them differently.
- **Exit intent** — detect when a cursor moves toward the browser close button and fire a last-chance offer before they leave.
- **Time of day** — show morning visitors a different CTA to evening visitors. Run time-specific promotions without touching your page.
- **Day** — run a weekend-only deal. Change your headline on Mondays. Create calendar-driven personalisation with no manual work.
- **Referrer URL** — detect which specific page they came from, including competitor sites, partner blogs, or specific ad landing pages.
- **Browser** — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge. Useful for tech-specific messaging or troubleshooting browser-specific layout.
- **Operating system** — Windows, Mac, iOS, Android, Linux. Write to their world.
- **Visitor timezone** — serve timezone-aware urgency without a developer. "Offer ends at midnight your time" — and it actually does.

---

**7 Actions — Change Anything On The Page Without Touching Code**

> When a rule fires, PagePersona executes the action instantly — no page reload, no flicker, no delay.

- **Swap text** — replace any headline, subheadline, body copy, button label, or CTA with personalised copy. The visitor sees your version. The page code stays the same.
- **Swap image** — replace any image on the page with a different one. Product shots, hero images, testimonial photos — swap them for a visitor who needs to see something different.
- **Swap URL** — change where any link goes. Send Facebook traffic to a Facebook-specific landing page. Send email traffic to a checkout page that skips the pitch they've already read.
- **Hide section** — make any element invisible for specific visitors. Remove irrelevant offers, hide sections that don't apply, strip out distractions for high-intent visitors.
- **Show element** — reveal a hidden element for a specific visitor type. A VIP section for returning visitors. A bonus offer for email subscribers. A case study that only loads for visitors from a specific industry.
- **Show popup** — trigger a fully designed popup with your copy, image, video embed, or countdown. Built and designed inside PagePersona — no third-party popup tool needed.
- **Insert countdown** — inject a live countdown timer anywhere on the page. Fixed-date timers for launches. Session-based timers that reset per visitor for evergreen urgency.

---

**Full Popup Builder — Designed Inside PagePersona**

> No extra software. No Zapier. No third-party popup tools. Design, write, and launch popups entirely inside PagePersona.

- 10 pre-built layout templates to start from
- 12 position options — centre modal, fullscreen, top bar, bottom bar, all four corners, slide-in from any side
- Text blocks, image blocks, button blocks, video embeds, two-column layouts
- Background colour, background image, border radius, padding, overlay
- Display delay, frequency controls (once per session, once per day, always), entry animations
- Dynamic country token — write "Exclusive offer for {country} visitors" and it renders their actual country
- Embed a countdown timer directly inside any popup

---

**Countdown Timers That Actually Work**

> Two types. Both built inside PagePersona and injected onto any page with a single action.

- **Fixed-date countdown** — counts to a specific date and time. Perfect for launch deadlines, limited-time offers, and event registration close dates.
- **Session countdown** — a per-visitor timer stored in the browser. Starts fresh for each new visitor. Persists across page reloads within the same session. The visitor who comes back to your page mid-session sees the timer exactly where they left off — not reset. Real urgency, not theatre.

## SECTION 23 — FAQ

**Q: Does PagePersona work on my platform?**
> If your platform allows you to add a script to the `<head>` of your page, PagePersona works on it. This includes WordPress, GoHighLevel, ClickFunnels, Shopify, Webflow, Systeme.io, Framer, and any HTML or static site. For WordPress specifically, there's a ready-to-install plugin with your script ID pre-embedded — install it in under 60 seconds.

**Q: Do I need to know how to code?**
> No. PagePersona was built specifically so that marketers and agency owners — not developers — can use it. You connect your page by pasting a URL. You pick elements by clicking on them in a live preview. You write conditions using a plain-English rule builder. There is no code to write, edit, or understand.

**Q: Will this slow down my page?**
> No. The PagePersona script is lightweight and loads asynchronously — it does not block your page from loading. Personalisation actions fire after the page has loaded and are executed instantly in the browser. Your page performance scores are not affected.

**Q: Will my visitors know the page is personalised?**
> No. From the visitor's perspective, the page simply loads the way it loads. There's no flash, no reload, no visible switch. The personalisation happens invisibly. Your original page code never changes — PagePersona operates as a layer on top.

**Q: What happens to my existing page after I install PagePersona?**
> Nothing changes until you create and activate a rule. Your page continues to display exactly as it does now for all visitors. Rules only fire when a visitor matches the conditions you've set. Until a rule matches a visitor, they see your original page unchanged.

**Q: Is there a monthly fee?**
> Not if you get access during the launch. This is a one-time price available for the launch window only. After the launch closes, PagePersona moves to a subscription model. There's no way to get the one-time deal after that point.
